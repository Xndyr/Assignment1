Project Idea

Overview

My project idea involves creating a game that informs people playing the game and spread awareness on one of the current pressing issues our society and perhaps the whole world is dealing with which is climate change. It will have a storyline and interactive parts that would encourage players to engage and be knowledgeable on the facts about the pressing issue of climate change in the world and how it affects our society currently and give a glimpse on things that might happen when its too late and the effects of climate change is irreversible. This game would be developed using game engines such as Unity but specifics would be discussed later on.

Motivation

I think this project will be useful and interesting due to the fact that climate change is a serious and pressing issue that is currently ongoing at this moment. With that being said, I believe that before its too late and the effects are irreversible it’s a good idea to spread awareness through a game that can be played by the audience, regardless of their age especially a younger audience as they are the next generation. According to NASA’s global climate change report, our planet has been getting an average surface temperature rising about 0.9 Celsius since the late 19th century. According to NASA’s global climate change report, our planet has been getting an average surface temperature rising about 0.9 Celsius since the late 19th century. This project would help point this out by showing players digitally what’s currently happening and what might happen in the future if this problem is not solved.

Description

What I’m thinking of the features that will be included in this game to fully achieve its intended purpose while providing players a solid gameplay experience is to firstly start in making a storyline that can be influenced by the actions of the player and can branch out to various events and endings. Every action they take inside the game world would influence the overall storyline of the game while along the way, they would be exposed to real world facts regarding climate change. For example, the player is given a scenario later on in the story where he or she would be faced with a decision where he or she must choose to either sacrifice something may it be relating to maybe refusing to build or take advantage of plastics or coal factories that spew out toxic and harmful chemicals in the atmosphere and rivers, something along those lines of scenarios or choose to ignore the warnings and information the game gives the player be it a cutscene of a news report or a NPC bringing it up earlier on which would drastically affect the outcome and storyline chosen by the player which would eventually lead to the aftermath of climate change which is of course based on imagination and a bit of fiction since thankfully we have not reached a critical point of no return regarding the full harmful effects of what climate change might bring but also a bit factual as well as scenarios on that would be based on the reports or findings of researchers who would construct a scenario of what will might happen based on what’s happening in the real world currently. The game will also feature a link to services or websites that further explains the severity and facts about climate change to further educate players on the prevalent topic.

Tools and Technologies

Firstly, to put this idea into fruition, hardware, software, and other tools and equipment is needed. I’m thinking utilising software such as Unity as mentioned and maybe unreal engine 4 for the game engine. If me and my would be team of developers would come up and decide what style or type the overall game is be it a 2-D or mainly 3-D with elements of 2-D incorporated with it, software such as photoshop is needed especially for 2-D and if we decide on 3-D models to use, 3-D modelling software such as Blender or Maya. Some of these have a premium version so that would probably be purchased as functionality will be limited behind that subscription or price so might as well take advantage of the full version rather than settling for the free version while missing out on the features that might be beneficial in making the game. With every software, a good hardware is needed if you want to run the software and programs without any hitch or problems so computers with average or standard specifications regarding its parts such as processors and the amount of memory and RAM it has is acceptable as I think although it will be good to have high end or expensive parts and components, it does not have to follow or utilise that unless my development team would run into problems initially and decide to upgrade.

Skills required

The skills required for this project includes developers involved including myself to have a background on I.T specifically in the software development and game design. As mentioned in the tools and technologies written, Unity or Unreal engine will be primarily used as the game engine and with it its programming language that is needed to be learnt and familiarise with in order for the game to function properly. This includes learning the skill and fundamentals of programming languages such as C#. As for the hardware there isn’t really anything special or high end needed in order for the game to be made and be functional, acceptable specifications such as decent RAM such as a minimum of 8 or 16 GB of it or an average or above average CPU etc. and a good SSD just to name a few which of course depends on which engine the game will be made and the modelling which mainly 3-D. In addition, screen monitors especially ones that have a decent to big size is preferable to be able to see and open up windows and references when making the game itself, having multiple monitors is however not required but welcomed. The feasibility finding the skill such as having a background in software development may be quite difficult as finding someone or people who can be reliable be it a veteran that has years of experience or a fresh graduate out of university. The software and hardware required is not really a problem since as mentioned, the game engine used is open for download for everyone and the hardware although might be a problem when finding specific components if needed if its not widely available or is popular.

Outcome

If this project would be successful and achieve its intended purpose of at least bringing the issue of climate change into the minds of players, information about it would be spread out with their friends, family or colleagues which is the optimal scenario which means that the game impacted and fulfilled its purpose as not only being a video game to be played for killing time but rather also a tool to educate people about the pressing issue. The original problem which is climate change itself has a much greater scale and can’t be solved by just a game or people just absorbing the information the game has given them and not doing anything about it. It takes the whole world to combat this problem and the game is there to serve as a stepping stone or a way of showing people how important it is to unite as one and combat and try to reduce even the most miniscule contributor of climate change such as using cars who spew smoke when there’s public transportation available or using plastic bags and discarding trash everywhere which will eventually have a huge impact on the problem itself if people are educated and act upon what they have learned through credible sources and of course the game itself which started out as an idea. This project idea of having a video game created for people although a bit of a niche market especially targeted to the younger generation where they are the ones who may provide the answer to the pressing issue of climate change if the older generation does not come up with effective solutions in the future. The game will inform players the events or scenarios that might happen not to scare them or put a sense of false future but to educate and impact them on the severity of the situation and how even their smallest ways could contribute to alleviate the problem.
